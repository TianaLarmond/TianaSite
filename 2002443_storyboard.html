PROFESSIONAL PAGE

My professional page contains my resume as well as my professional vision and mission statements for my career. As most resumes do, the page presents me address, contact information, a picture of me, brief information on my high school and current university, extracurricular activities and academic achievements.
Image, audio, video, heading, paragraph, head, body, table, center and font tags were all included in this webpage.


PERSONAL PAGE

My likes, dislikes, hobbies, and other interests are displayed on my personal page. Its goal is to introduce viewers to who I am by providing them with information about my personal life and feelings. It has a personal quote from someone I like, who also happens to be an actor and producer.
On this page I use internal, external and mailto links. I also include image, audio, video, heading, paragraph, head, body and font tags. Additionally, I include unordered lists, center and table tags.
